OMRA LAB | PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: December 27, 2025
Effective Date: December 27, 2025

1. DATA COLLECTION

OMRA Lab is a literary art project, not a commercial service. We collect minimal data necessary for group access management.

Information We Collect:

  • Telegram Username: Provided voluntarily via Tally.so form to receive group invite
  • Optional Note: Any additional information you choose to share in the access request form
  • Quiz Results: Pass/fail status (not linked to personally identifiable information)

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • Real names, email addresses, or phone numbers (unless you provide them voluntarily)
  • Tracking cookies or analytics
  • IP addresses or device information
  • Content of Telegram group messages (Telegram controls this data)

2. DATA USAGE

Your Telegram username is used solely to:

  • Send you a manual invitation link to the OMRA Lab Telegram group
  • Verify quiz completion status

We do NOT:

  • Sell, rent, or share your information with third parties
  • Use your data for marketing or advertising
  • Track your activity outside of the Telegram group

3. DATA STORAGE & RETENTION

Tally.so Form Submissions: Stored by Tally.so (third-party service). See Tally Privacy Policy.

Telegram Group: Messages and user data managed by Telegram. See Telegram Privacy Policy.

Retention: We delete access request data after processing (typically 48-72 hours). Telegram group membership data is controlled by Telegram's systems.

4. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request what information we hold about you (email: lab@404soul.ru)
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your Telegram username from our records
  • Leave: Exit the Telegram group at any time without explanation
  • Anonymity: Use pseudonyms; we do not verify real identities

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

OMRA Lab uses the following third-party platforms:

  • GitHub Pages: Hosts this website (no personal data collected)
  • Teleform: Hosts the screening quiz (anonymous responses)
  • Tally.so: Collects access request forms
  • Telegram: Hosts the recovery group

Each service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them.

OMRA Lab is a literary art project and peer documentation space.

We do NOT:

  • Provide therapy, counseling, or medical advice
  • Replace professional mental health treatment
  • Guarantee any psychological outcomes

7. SECURITY

We implement reasonable security measures:

  • Manual approval process prevents automated scraping
  • No public database of usernames
  • HTTPS encryption for website access
  • Telegram's end-to-end encryption for group messages

However, no system is 100% secure. By participating, you accept this inherent risk.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

OMRA Lab is intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has accessed the system, contact us immediately.
